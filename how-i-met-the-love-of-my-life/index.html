How I met the LOVE of my Life

09 November 2014

Blues on The Green

It was a Wednesday when I first drove down, Waco to Austin a relatively short drive. I will always tell her that I wasn't nervous, but I'm sure there were some butterflies. I thought about what I was going to say and what I was going to do, would I be an awkard squirrel or talk to much.

Of course I would get lost. Why not. How simple is it to follow maps to an address? Apparently not that simple. I had to call her, but this had to be the first time we will talk on the phone. The phone call went like this.

Aaron: "Hello"
Alyssa: "Ugh Hi"
Aaron: "I'm lost, Can you help me"
Alyssa: "Laugh"
Alyssa: "Laugh"
Alyssa: "I'll be right out!"

Guys conceding on not knowing directions. Especially on the first date? Of course. My life.
